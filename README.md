ION EV DISDAIN:
I'm trying to build a new type of ion engine inspired by the aerospike engine i have experience in building ion propulsion engines but this is nothing like what ive built in the past i hope that till the end of this project ive perfected the new funnel system that i made for it.

WHAT DOES IT DO?
well my friend called it a glorified fan with lights but i oppose the idea, i have long tried to find a new alternative to mechanical engines and ion is the way to go this engine creates THRUST WITH NO MOVING PARTS! it does so by ionizing the air and accelerating the charged particles to generate ionic wind or thrust! its a neat concept and even cooler once built and i really think it will develop into an actual usable engine especially for ssto's.

i really love propulsion engineering so it was natural that i stumbled upon ion engines but what really made me want to build this were the starships from star trek THEY HAVE SUCH COOL DESIGNS PLUS THE AFTERBURNER OR whatever you would call subsonic blue mirages in space were so cool and as it turns out ion engines emit a faint purple on the ionizing edge and its SO COOL AGHHHH i designed my big engine hunk to look like a nacelle from the uss discovery and enterprise hhahaha
